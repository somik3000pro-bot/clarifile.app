Clarifile Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Clarifile helps you scan, translate, and understand documents. This policy explains what information Clarifile collects, how it is used, and how you can delete your account and data.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Clarifile, we may process or store the following information:

  • Account information, such as your email address and user identifier.
  • Subscription status and purchase history needed to provide paid features.
  • Document images, imported files, extracted text, translations, document titles, and optional AI interpretation results.
  • Usage records such as monthly scan, OCR, translation, storage, and quota counters.
  • Technical information needed to authenticate requests, secure the service, and operate cloud features.

Camera and Photo Library

Clarifile asks for camera access so you can scan documents. It may also ask for photo library or file access so you can import document images or PDFs. Document images and extracted text may contain personal information from the documents you choose to scan or import.

How Documents Are Processed

Clarifile uses on-device OCR first where available. If OCR quality is low, or if you request translation or AI interpretation, document images or text may be sent through secure server functions to service providers for processing.

Free and Premium Storage

On the free plan, saved documents are stored on your device. OCR and translation may still require network processing. Premium users may back up document images, extracted text, translations, and interpretations to their Clarifile cloud account for cross-device access.

Service Providers

Clarifile uses third-party service providers to operate the app:

  • Supabase for authentication, database, storage, server functions, usage records, and cloud document backup.
  • OpenAI for cloud OCR fallback and optional AI document interpretation.
  • DeepL and Google Translate for translation.
  • RevenueCat, Apple, and Google for subscription status and purchase processing.

Some service providers, including OpenAI and RevenueCat, process data in the United States. Transfers are made under standard contractual clauses or other lawful transfer mechanisms.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide authentication, OCR, translation, AI interpretation, document storage, subscription access, account deletion, quota enforcement, service reliability, and customer support. We do not sell your personal information.

We process your information on the basis of contract performance — processing is necessary to provide the Clarifile service you requested. Where you grant camera or file access, processing is based on your consent, which you can withdraw by revoking permissions in iOS Settings.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your Clarifile account from Settings in the app. Account deletion removes your Clarifile cloud documents, stored files, subscription metadata, usage data, and local cached Clarifile documents, then signs you out. Some payment records may remain with Apple, Google, RevenueCat, or other processors as required for billing, tax, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

Security

Clarifile uses authentication, access controls, and secure transport to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so only scan or upload documents you are comfortable processing through the app.

Children

Clarifile is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, contact us at support@clarifile.app and we will delete it.

Your Choices

You can choose not to grant camera or file access, but scanning and import features may not work. You can manage subscriptions through Apple App Store or Google Play account settings. You can request help or data questions through support.

We will update the date at the top of this page when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@clarifile.app.
